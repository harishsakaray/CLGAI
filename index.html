Privacy Policy for Cover Letter Generator

Last updated: 03/04/2025

Introduction

This privacy policy explains how the Cover Letter Generator Chrome extension collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the extension. As an individual developer, I respect your privacy and am committed to protecting your personal information.

Data Collection

This extension collects the following data:

  • Website Content: The extension extracts job details (job title, company name, location, and job description) from LinkedIn job postings that you are viewing.
  • Resume Content: The extension processes resume content that you upload to generate personalized cover letters.

Data Usage

The data collected by the extension is used for the following purposes:

  • To generate personalized cover letters by matching your resume content with job posting details
  • To improve the functionality and user experience of the extension

Data Storage and Security

This extension:

  • Stores data locally within your browser using browser storage technologies
  • Does not transmit your data to external servers
  • Only processes your data on your device
  • Does not retain your data after you close the extension or browser

Third-Party Access

I do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your data to third parties. All processing happens locally on your device.

User Control

You have control over your data. You can:

  • Choose when to activate the extension
  • Clear your browser's local storage to remove any stored data

Updates to This Policy

I may update this privacy policy from time to time. You will be notified of any changes through the extension's update process.
